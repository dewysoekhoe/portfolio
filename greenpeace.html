ASSIGNMENT

For this assignment, I found an organization that touched me and uses volunteers. For this, I will develop a product that I can recruit. The digital prototypes is made in InVision. When I read the assignment, I was equal to big organizations like Unicef, Amnesty International and the WNF. Unfortunately, I did not get any ideas for a product. After a few days, my brother and I talk about global warming and the sea level. I do not even know that I was interested, but decided to take this as a subject because I could continue this conversation.

Soon I came to Greenpeace, an international organization that wants to draw attention to large-scale environmental problems. After I chose the organization, I began to think about the interactive product. I thought of how to attract people who know nothing or little about Greenpeace and who want to become the same volunteers. After this question, I began to wonder why the global warming makes me do something. I found out that I was so liable because I was silent by the fact that humanity causes sea level to rise, or at least accelerate.

After that, I thought about how to educate and educate teachers about becoming a Greenpeace adviser. Immediately I thought of a movie were the earth would be destroyed. I think you can draw the best attention by showing something shocking. What strengthens this experience is an interactive 3D movie that you can view with a virtual reality color. My target group is secondary school teachers who would like to contribute to improving our climate. They will do this by providing information about groups and being creative. The chosen event is TEACHING MAKE THE DIFFERENCE! What will take place on May 24, 2017 in Beatrixtheater, Jaarbeursplein, Utrecht.

At the end I spend too much time on making the animation, that I forgot about the interaction. Unfortunately I had to fake my installation and I was told to take the VR experience out of my concept.
