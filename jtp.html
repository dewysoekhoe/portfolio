ASSIGNMENT

With the Beyond project and the associated courses I have learned to design for a multi-device and multi-user context. This means that I have learned to research, substantiate and solve a complex design challenge. I did this within a team, where I draw from each other's strength but also give space to each other's ambition.

My team was not very motivated at the beginning. As the period progressed we have managed to distribute a lot of tasks as a team. I also noticed that when we got closer to the presentations, everyone was working professionally on his part and luckily this was made up for. We had not established anyone within the team who would take the lead.

What I will do next time is to go deeper into the assignments and put more time into them to understand them faster, so that I can get off to a better start when carrying out the methods. I think that I am now able to conduct a better research in the future.
