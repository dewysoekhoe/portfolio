About

ASSIGNMENT

Looking back on this project, I found the collaboration to be good. Elvira Berkout took the initiative to contact Luuk Visser and me to team up together.

I have learned how the process goes, in order to make an interactive product for a user, goes into its operation. There is much more to look than I thought, like the wishes of a person, company and technology. I have also learned to converge and diverge often in the design process.

I liked to design the prototype for Meet for Coffee. The design made me easy, while communicating the concept took a little longer to work out. Through this project, I gained a lot of experience about creating an interactive product product for a user. I learned in the user, analyzed questions and elaborated a concept for an interactive product.
