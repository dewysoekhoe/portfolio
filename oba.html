ASSIGNMENT

I have designed the layout of the screens for the OBA app. The visual user experience is very important when designing. Through the Principles of User Interface Design and the lessons I learned how to apply shapes, size, color, text, typography, hierarchy and animations to a design.

I felt constantly challenged in various assignments to investigate and experiment by making and doing. I have learned to tell a message in form, to find creative solutions for complex problems and to visualize, substantiate and transfer these solutions.
