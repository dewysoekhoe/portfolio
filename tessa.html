About

ASSIGNMENT

Going into this team project I knew that I would take the lead. Most of the time I am the leader, but in this project I was not the only one that was thinking like me. Luuk Visser and I kind of both took the lead. This was a new experience for me, because I am not used to that. But it was deffinitely a good idea to put us together. We both have a strong vision and we both like to be heard.

There were times were it was diffucult for to let go for a moment and let the others fix some of our problems. But at the end it came along all good. We have designed an interactive product that supports sleep and falling asleep. Our product is called TESSA.

The name is derivative from Interstellar, when I heard the assignment I directly thaught of that movie. There was this vision in my head of a five dimensional product. After this idea we began to sketch and eventually we had our concept.

The purpose of our product was that you as a user fall asleep easier and finer and that the quality of your sleep is better. In addition, it is necessary that the smartphone disappears as much as possible from the bedroom. Because of TESSA there are no reasons to have smartphone in your bedroom. TESSA will record your sleep and will registrate this for you. Website: https://oege.ie.hva.nl/~soekhod002/TESSA/TESSA%20one%20pager/
